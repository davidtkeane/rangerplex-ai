## ğŸ¯ Pro Tips

### Speed Drawing

**Fast workflow**:
1. Press `P` for pen
2. Draw your sketch
3. Press `H` for highlighter
4. Emphasize key points
5. Press `Ctrl+S` to save
6. Press `Esc` to close

**Total time**: ~30 seconds from start to finish!

---

### Quick Corrections

**Fix mistakes fast**:
1. Press `E` for eraser
2. Remove mistake
3. Press `P` to return to pen
4. Continue drawing

**Or**:
1. Press `Ctrl+Z` to undo
2. Redraw correctly

---

### Efficient Note-Taking

**Keyboard-first workflow**:
1. Open canvas (click tab or type "canvas" in chat)
2. Press `P` for pen (if not already selected)
3. Draw/write notes
4. Press `H` to highlight important parts
5. Press `Ctrl+S` when done
6. Press `Esc` to close

**No mouse needed!** (except for drawing)
